# Sample Requirements — Highway Bridge Structural Design

## Structural Load Requirements

- Req-1: The bridge deck must support AASHTO HL-93 live load configuration without exceeding allowable stress.
- Req-2: The main girder deflection must not exceed L/800 under maximum service load.
- Req-3: The pier columns must resist a 5.0 magnitude seismic event with ductility ratio >= 3.0.
- Req-4: The foundation must sustain ultimate bearing capacity of 300 kPa with a safety factor of 2.5.

## Material Requirements

- Req-5: All structural steel must meet ASTM A709 Grade 50W weathering steel specifications.
- Req-6: Concrete must achieve minimum compressive strength of 35 MPa at 28 days per ACI 318.
- Req-7: Reinforcement must use Grade 60 deformed bars with minimum 40mm clear cover.

## Environmental and Durability Requirements

- Req-8: The drainage system must handle 100-year storm intensity without deck ponding exceeding 25mm.
- Req-9: The bridge must be designed for a 75-year service life with corrosion protection system.
- Req-10: Expansion joints must accommodate thermal movement of +/-50mm without leakage.

## Inspection Requirements

- Req-11: The structure must include embedded sensors for real-time deflection and vibration monitoring.
- Req-12: Access walkways must be provided beneath the deck for visual inspection of all structural members.
